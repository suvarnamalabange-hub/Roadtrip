Medical Emergency Steps

1
Stay Calm
Take deep breaths. Panic can worsen the situation.
2
Call 108/112
Immediately call ambulance services with exact location.
3
Provide First Aid
Only if trained. Otherwise, wait for professional help.
4
Contact Family
Inform emergency contacts about the situation.
