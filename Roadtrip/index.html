Road Trip Guide

Essential Road Trip Checklist

Vehicle Essentials

  • Spare Tyre & Puncture Kit
  • Jumper Cables & Tow Rope
  • Coolant & Engine Oil
  • Basic Toolkit (Wrenches, etc.)
  • Jerry Can for extra fuel (for Ladakh)

Documents & Finances

  • RC, License, PUC, Insurance
  • Inner Line Permits (for Ladakh)
  • ID Proof (Aadhaar, etc.)
  • Cash (for remote areas)
  • FASTag for tolls

Personal Items & Clothing

  • Layered Clothing (Thermals, Fleece)
  • Rain Gear & Windcheater
  • Comfortable Shoes/Boots
  • Sunscreen, Sunglasses & Hat
  • Basic Toiletries

Health & Safety

  • First-Aid Kit with essentials
  • Altitude Sickness Medicine
  • Pain Relievers & Antiseptics
  • Hand Sanitizer & Masks
  • High-energy snacks & Water

Tech & Gadgets

  • Power Bank & Car Charger
  • Offline Maps (e.g., Google Maps)
  • Phone Mount for car
  • Camera & Extra Batteries
  • Postpaid SIM (better connectivity)

Traveler's Code: Safety & Etiquette

Mountain Driving

  • Avoid driving at night
  • Honk on blind curves
  • Give way to uphill traffic
  • Check brakes frequently

Cultural Etiquette

  • Dress modestly at temples
  • Remove footwear before entry
  • Ask before photographing people
  • Respect rituals & silence

General Health

  • Stay hydrated always
  • Eat at busy, clean places
  • Be aware of your surroundings
  • Keep emergency contacts handy
